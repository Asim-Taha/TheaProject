Press event

Participate in the upcoming FIFA 23 tournament and win INSANE cash prizes ranging from $100,000 all the way to $500,000. Open participation for anyone within Canada.

EA Sports - All the great FIFA 23 players in Canada have been invited to participate in the upcoming FIFA tournament. This tournament aims to bring the best players of FIFA 23 in North America in public eyes and award them for being a valued player in our community. EA Sports is partnering with the FIFA association, Adidas and Sony to bring this tournament to life and show the whole world our wonderful community, players and game itself.

This is an open Tournament for all Canadian FIFA 23 players where they will compete against each other while in the comfort of their home. The tournament will have a point based system for the first two weeks where the goal for each player would be to gain as much points as they can get. After that, we will take 32 players with the most points and have them compete in a grand tournament.

The winner of the Grand Tournament will tin $500,000, second place will get $200,000 and third place will get $100,000.

The qualifier tournament will start on February 15th until March 1st and you will be able to play in that tournament from your home. After March 1st, EA Sports will personally contact you and fly you out to Toronto Ontario where you will stay for 3 days and participate in the 2 day event.

If you do not participate or fail to qualify but would like to watch the the grand tournament anyways, you can do so on your FIFA 23 where we will stream the tournament or you can watch the event the EA Sports website
